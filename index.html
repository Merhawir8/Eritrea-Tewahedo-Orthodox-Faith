Welcome to the Eritrean Tewahedo Faith!

The Eritrean Tewahedo Orthodox church has similar doctrine believe as the other oriental orthodox such as the Coptic orthodox and Ethiopian Tewahedo Orthodox church. The name Tewahedo refers to the unity of Christ's identity as he was fully human and fully God. We believe in the five pilliars of mysteries. They are the mystery of the Trinity, mystery of the Incarnation, mystery of the Baptism, mystery of the Communion, and the mystery of Resurrection.
